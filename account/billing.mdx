## Free Trial

All plans include a **7-day free trial**. A credit card is required to start — you won't be charged until the trial ends. Cancel any time before day 7 and you pay nothing.

At the end of your trial, you're automatically moved to your selected paid plan. Stripe sends a reminder email on day 5.

## Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Profile → Billing** or click **Upgrade** in the sidebar
2. Select a plan
3. Confirm — your new plan activates immediately with a prorated charge for the current cycle

## Downgrading or Cancelling

1. Go to **Profile → Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan** or **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm

Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. Papers and chat history are preserved.

## Payment Methods

EssayGen uses **Stripe** for all payment processing:
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- Apple Pay and Google Pay (where available)

Your card details are stored securely by Stripe — never on EssayGen's servers.

## Invoices

Invoices are emailed after each payment and downloadable from **Profile → Billing → Invoice History**.

## Word Count and Overages

EssayGen does **not** charge overages. Generation pauses at 100% usage until your cycle resets or you upgrade.

Usage warnings appear at:
- **70%** — yellow warning in the Dashboard
- **90%** — red warning
- **100%** — generation paused
