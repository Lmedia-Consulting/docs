## Overview

Your Profile page has six tabs covering every aspect of your account settings.

## Overview Tab

Shows a summary of your account:
- Name, email, and profile photo
- Current plan and renewal date
- Quick usage summary
- Account preferences

## Usage Tab

Detailed breakdown of your word usage:
- Current month used / total
- Per-feature breakdown (Writer, Chat)
- Monthly usage history chart
- Paper count stats

<Note>
  Word counts reset at the start of each billing cycle. Unused words do not roll over.
</Note>

## Appearance Tab

Customize how EssayGen looks:
- **Theme** — Light, Dark, or System default
- **Color Accent** — Choose an accent color for the UI

Appearance settings sync across devices.

## Billing Tab

Manage your subscription:
- Current plan and pricing
- Next billing date
- Payment method on file
- Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel options
- Invoice history

[More details in the Billing guide →](/account/billing)

## Security Tab

- **Two-factor authentication (2FA)** — Enable TOTP-based 2FA
- **Active sessions** — View and revoke active login sessions
- **Password** — Change your password (if using email login)

## Notifications Tab

Control which emails you receive:
- Generation complete notifications
- Weekly usage digest
- Product updates and announcements
- Billing and renewal reminders

Toggle each type on or off. Changes save automatically.
