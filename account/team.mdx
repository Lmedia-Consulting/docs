<Note>
  Team features are available on **Pro ($49.99/mo)** and **Business+ ($79.99/mo)** plans.
</Note>

## Overview

The Team feature lets you add collaborators to your EssayGen workspace. Teammates share your plan's word allowance and access features based on their assigned role.

## Inviting Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team">
    Click **Team** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite a member">
    Click **Invite Member**, enter their email address, select their role, and click **Send Invite**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They accept the invite">
    The invitee receives an email. Once accepted, they can log in and access EssayGen under your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Roles and Permissions

| Role | Access |
|---|---|
| Admin | Full access — invite/remove members, view all content, manage settings |
| Member | Generate and view their own content; cannot manage team settings |
| Viewer | Read-only access to shared content |

## Shared Word Allowance

All team members share the plan's monthly word allowance. Monitor usage from **Profile → Usage** to see per-member breakdowns.

## Removing a Member

1. Go to **Team**
2. Click **⋯** next to the member's name
3. Select **Remove from Team**

Removed members immediately lose access. Their previously generated content remains in the account.
