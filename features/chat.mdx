## Overview

The Chat feature gives you a persistent AI assistant with specialized modes for writing, research, and business content generation. Switch between modes depending on your task.

## Session Limits

| Plan | Chat sessions/month |
|---|---|
| Starter | 50 sessions |
| Growth | Unlimited |
| Pro | Unlimited |

A session is a single conversation thread. You can have as many exchanges within a session as you need.

## Starting a Chat

1. Click **Chat** in the sidebar
2. Select a mode from the dropdown
3. Type your message and press **Enter**

Chat history is saved automatically. Access previous threads from the history panel on the left.

## Tips for Best Results

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Research mode before writing">
    Ask Chat to find sources and summarize research before generating a paper. Copy the key points into your Writer prompt for more accurate output.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use Essay mode for feedback">
    Paste a draft paragraph and ask: "Make this more persuasive" or "Rewrite this at a graduate level."
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use SEO mode for content briefs">
    Give SEO mode your target keyword and ask for a full content brief with H2 structure, word count recommendation, and related terms to include.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use Brainstorm for outlines">
    Give your topic and ask for a 5-point outline with supporting arguments. Use that as the basis for your Writer prompt.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Chat History

All conversations are saved to your account. From the history panel you can:
- Continue any past conversation
- Rename threads for organization
- Delete conversations you no longer need
