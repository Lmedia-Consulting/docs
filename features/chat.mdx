## Overview

The Chat feature gives you a persistent AI assistant with six specialized modes. Use it to research topics, outline papers, brainstorm arguments, study concepts, or practice debate.

## Chat Modes

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="General" icon="comments">
    Open-ended assistant for any writing question or task.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Essay" icon="pen-nib">
    Focused on essay structure, arguments, thesis development, and writing feedback.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Research" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Finds and summarizes sources, explains academic concepts, and pulls citations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Brainstorm" icon="lightbulb">
    Generates topic ideas, angles, counterarguments, and outlines.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Study" icon="graduation-cap">
    Explains difficult material, creates study guides, and quizzes you on concepts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Debate" icon="scale-balanced">
    Builds arguments and counterarguments for both sides of a topic.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Starting a Chat

1. Click **Chat** in the sidebar
2. Select a mode from the dropdown at the top
3. Type your message and press **Enter** or click **Send**

Your chat history is saved automatically. Each conversation thread is accessible from the chat sidebar.

## Tips for Best Results

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Research mode before writing">
    Ask Chat to find sources and summarize research before you generate a paper. Copy the key points into your Writer prompt for better, more accurate output.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use Essay mode for feedback">
    Paste a draft paragraph into Essay mode and ask: "Make this more persuasive" or "Rewrite this at a graduate level."
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use Brainstorm for outlines">
    Give Brainstorm mode your topic and ask for a 5-point outline with supporting arguments. Use that as the basis for your Writer prompt.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Chat History

All conversations are saved to your account. Access previous threads from the history panel on the left side of the Chat page. You can:

- Continue any past conversation
- Rename threads for organization
- Delete conversations you no longer need
