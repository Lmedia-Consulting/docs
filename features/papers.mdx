## Overview

The Papers Library is your central hub for all content you've generated with EssayGen. Every completed paper or document is saved automatically.

## Navigating the Library

Papers are displayed as cards showing:
- **Title** and topic
- **Content type** and writing level
- **Word count**
- **Humanization score** (0–100)
- **Status** (Complete / In Progress / Failed)
- **Date created**

### Filtering and Search

| Tool | Options |
|---|---|
| Search | Search by title or topic keyword |
| Filter | All, Complete, In Progress, Failed |
| Sort | Newest, Oldest, Highest Score, Lowest Score |

## Viewing a Paper

Click any paper card to open the full view. From here you can:

- **Read** the full content
- **Edit** in the built-in text editor
- **Download** as a DOCX file
- **View** the humanization score breakdown
- **Regenerate** if you want a fresh version

## Humanization Scores

The score shown on each paper is an aggregate estimate based on multiple AI detection models.

- **85–100:** Excellent — very likely to pass AI detectors
- **70–84:** Good — may trigger some detectors in edge cases
- **Below 70:** Consider editing manually or regenerating

<Tip>
  Small personal touches significantly improve scores — specific examples, changed transitions, varied sentence lengths, and your own voice where possible.
</Tip>
