## Overview

The Writer is EssayGen's unified generation engine. It handles everything from academic essays to long-form business documents — including RFIs, RFPs, SEO articles, blog posts, and professional reports — all in one place.

## Generating Content

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Writer">
    Click **Writer** in the sidebar or **Generate Paper** from the Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your topic or prompt">
    Type your topic or a detailed prompt. The more specific, the better the output.

    **Academic:** *"Argumentative essay on social media's impact on mental health, 1,500 words, APA 7"*

    **RFP:** *"RFP response for a cloud infrastructure modernization project, 8,000 words, formal tone"*

    **SEO:** *"SEO article targeting 'best project management software for startups', 1,800 words"*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your options">
    | Option | Description |
    |---|---|
    | Content Type | Academic essay, research paper, RFI, RFP, SEO article, blog post, business report, email, and more |
    | Writing Level / Tone | High School → PhD (academic) or Formal / Conversational / Persuasive (business) |
    | Word Count | 500 – 15,000+ words |
    | Citation Style | APA 7, MLA 9, Chicago, Harvard (academic content) |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate**. Content streams in real time. Short pieces (500–1,000 words) take ~30–60 seconds. Long-form documents (RFPs at 8,000+ words) take 2–4 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and download">
    Edit in the built-in editor, then save to your library or download as DOCX.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Humanization

All generated content is automatically processed through EssayGen's humanization pipeline, which:

- Rewrites AI-patterned sentences with natural variation
- Mixes writing fingerprints from multiple AI models to reduce detector confidence
- Targets a humanization score above 85

The humanization score is shown after generation and in your Papers library.

<Note>
  Humanization scores are estimates based on AI detection models (Sapling, GPTZero, Originality.ai). No tool can guarantee a 100% human score on all detectors at all times.
</Note>

## Citations and Sources

For academic content and research-heavy business documents, EssayGen sources real references via Perplexity's research API. Citations are formatted to your selected style with in-text references and a bibliography.

For RFPs and RFIs, Tavily web research is used to inject current, relevant context into the generation.

| Plan | Research quality |
|---|---|
| Essential | Standard sourcing |
| Pro | Enhanced research (Perplexity Sonar Pro + Tavily) |
| Business+ | Deep research mode (Perplexity Sonar Deep Research) |
