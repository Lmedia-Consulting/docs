## Overview

The Writer is EssayGen's core generation tool. It handles everything from academic essays and research papers to business reports, proposals, and marketing copy — all in one place.

## Generating Content

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Writer">
    Click **Writer** in the sidebar or **Generate Paper** from the Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your topic or prompt">
    Type your topic or a detailed prompt. Be specific for better results.

    **Academic example:** *"Argumentative essay on the impact of social media on mental health, 1500 words, APA"*

    **Business example:** *"Executive summary for a Q2 marketing report, formal tone, 500 words"*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your options">
    | Option | Description |
    |---|---|
    | Content Type | Academic essay, research paper, business report, proposal, email, marketing copy, and more |
    | Writing Level | High School, Undergraduate, Graduate, PhD (academic) or Formal / Conversational (business) |
    | Word Count | 500 – 5,000+ words |
    | Citation Style | APA 7, MLA 9, Chicago, Harvard (academic content) |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate**. Your content streams in real time. A 1,000-word piece takes roughly 30–60 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and download">
    Edit in the built-in editor, then save to your library or download as DOCX.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Humanization

Every generated piece is automatically processed through EssayGen's humanization pipeline, which:

- Rewrites AI-patterned sentences with natural variation
- Mixes writing fingerprints from multiple AI models to reduce detector confidence
- Targets a humanization score above 85

The humanization score is shown after generation and in your Papers library.

<Note>
  Humanization scores are estimates based on AI detection models (Sapling, GPTZero, Originality.ai). No tool can guarantee a 100% human score on all detectors at all times.
</Note>

## Citations and Sources

For academic content, EssayGen sources real references via Perplexity's research API. Citations are formatted to your selected style with in-text references and a bibliography.

| Plan | Research quality |
|---|---|
| Essential | Standard sourcing |
| Pro | Enhanced research (Perplexity Sonar Pro) |
| Business+ | Deep research mode (Perplexity Sonar Deep Research) |
