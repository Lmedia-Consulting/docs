## 1. Create an account

Go to [essaygen.ai](https://essaygen.ai) and click **Get Started**. Sign up with your email or Google account.

After sign-up you'll land on your **Dashboard** where you can see your usage, recent papers, and quick-action cards.

## 2. Generate your first essay

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Writer">
    From your Dashboard, click **Generate Paper** or navigate to **Writer** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your topic and options">
    Enter your essay topic, then choose:
    - **Paper type** — Argumentative, Analytical, Research, etc.
    - **Writing level** — High School, College, Graduate
    - **Word count** — 500 to 5,000+ words
    - **Citation style** — APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and review">
    Click **Generate**. Your essay will stream in real time. Once complete, review it in the editor and make any edits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or save">
    Save the paper to your library or download it as a DOCX file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 3. Check your usage

Your current word usage is shown in the Dashboard's usage meter. You'll see a warning at 70% capacity and a final warning at 90%.

<Note>
  Word count resets at the start of each billing cycle. Upgrade your plan anytime from **Profile → Billing**.
</Note>
