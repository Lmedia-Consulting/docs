## 1. Create your account

Go to [essaygen.ai](https://essaygen.ai) and click **Get Started**.

- **Starter plan** — includes a 7-day free trial (card required, no charge until day 8)
- **Growth and Pro** — billed immediately, no trial

After sign-up you'll land on your **Dashboard**.

## 2. Generate your first piece of content

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Writer">
    Click **Writer** in the sidebar or **Generate Paper** from the Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your topic and set options">
    Type your topic or prompt, then choose:
    - **Content type** — essay, SEO article, blog post, RFP, business report, etc.
    - **Writing level / tone** — High School through PhD (academic) or Formal / Conversational (business)
    - **Word count** — 500 to 15,000+ words
    - **Citation style** — APA 7, MLA 9, Chicago, Harvard (academic content)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and review">
    Click **Generate**. Content streams in real time. Once complete, review it in the editor and make any edits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or save">
    Save to your library or download as DOCX.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 3. Check your usage

Your word usage is shown in the Dashboard's usage meter. You'll see warnings at 70% and 90% capacity.

<Note>
  Word count resets at the start of each billing cycle. Unused words do not roll over. Upgrade anytime from **Profile → Billing**.
</Note>
