---
title: "FAQ"
description: "Answers to the most common questions about EssayGen"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is EssayGen detectable as AI?">
    EssayGen applies multi-layer humanization to all generated content, targeting scores above 85 on AI detection tools. However, no tool can guarantee 100% undetectability on all detectors at all times — detection models are updated frequently. For best results, review and lightly personalize generated content before submitting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What citation styles does EssayGen support?">
    EssayGen supports APA 7, MLA 9, Chicago (Notes-Bibliography and Author-Date), and Harvard. Citation style selection is available in the Writer and applies to both in-text citations and the bibliography.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How accurate are the sources and citations?">
    EssayGen uses Perplexity's academic search API to find real sources. Citations reference real papers and articles. Always verify sources before submitting academic work — AI can occasionally misattribute citation details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I hit my word limit?">
    Generation pauses when you reach 100% of your monthly word allowance. Your limit resets at the start of each billing cycle. You can upgrade at any time to get more words immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit generated content?">
    Yes. All generated content opens in a built-in text editor. You can edit any part before downloading. Changes are saved automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file formats can I download?">
    Currently, papers can be downloaded as **DOCX** files compatible with Microsoft Word and Google Docs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does EssayGen support languages other than English?">
    EssayGen is optimized for English-language content. Other languages may work depending on the underlying model but are not officially supported.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    Go to **Profile → Billing → Cancel Subscription**. Your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period. No data is deleted upon cancellation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my content private?">
    Yes. Your generated papers and chat conversations are private to your account. EssayGen does not share or train on your content without consent. See the [Privacy Policy](https://essaygen.ai/privacy) for full details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What AI models does EssayGen use?">
    EssayGen uses multiple models including Anthropic Claude (primary generation and chat) and Perplexity (research sourcing). The specific model used depends on your plan and the task type.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
