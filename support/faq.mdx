---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What plans does EssayGen offer?">
    EssayGen has three plans:
    - **Starter** ($24.99/mo) — for individuals, students, and solo writers. 30,000 words/month.
    - **Growth** ($49.99/mo) — for freelancers, bloggers, and content marketers. 100,000 words/month + RFI/RFP generation.
    - **Pro** ($149/mo) — for agencies, consultants, and B2B teams. 300,000 words/month + API access + Brand Books.

    All plans include a 7-day free trial. Save 33% with annual billing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Yes — all plans include a 7-day free trial. A credit card is required to start. You won't be charged until day 8. Cancel any time before then and you pay nothing. You'll receive a reminder email on day 5.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is EssayGen detectable as AI?">
    EssayGen applies multi-layer humanization to all generated content, targeting scores above 85 on AI detection tools. However, no tool can guarantee 100% undetectability on all detectors at all times — detection models are updated frequently. For best results, review and lightly personalize generated content before submitting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What citation styles does EssayGen support?">
    APA 7, MLA 9, Chicago (Notes-Bibliography and Author-Date), and Harvard. Citation style is set in the Writer and applies to both in-text citations and the bibliography.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How accurate are the sources and citations?">
    EssayGen uses Perplexity's academic search API to find real sources. Always verify citations before submitting academic work — AI can occasionally misattribute details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a document credit?">
    Document credits are used for high-cost long-form generation (RFPs and RFIs). Growth includes 5 credits/month, Pro includes 20. Credits are separate from your word count allowance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I hit my word limit?">
    Generation pauses when you reach 100% of your monthly word allowance. Your limit resets at the start of each billing cycle. Upgrade any time to get more words immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit generated content?">
    Yes. All generated content opens in a built-in text editor. Changes save automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file formats can I download?">
    All content can be downloaded as **DOCX** files, compatible with Microsoft Word and Google Docs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which plans include RFP and RFI generation?">
    RFP and RFI generation is available on **Growth** (5/month) and **Pro** (20/month). It is not available on Starter.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does EssayGen support languages other than English?">
    EssayGen is optimized for English. Other languages may work depending on the underlying model but are not officially supported.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    Go to **Profile → Billing → Cancel Subscription**. Your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period. No data is deleted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my content private?">
    Yes. Your papers and conversations are private to your account. EssayGen does not share or train on your content without consent. See the [Privacy Policy](https://essaygen.ai/privacy) for full details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
