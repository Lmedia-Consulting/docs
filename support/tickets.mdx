## Submitting a Ticket

The fastest way to get help is through the in-app support system.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Support">
    Click **Support** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Ticket">
    Fill in:
    - **Subject** — brief description of your issue
    - **Category** — Billing, Technical, Account, Feature Request, or Other
    - **Description** — as much detail as possible
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit**. You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket number.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Ticket Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Open | Your ticket has been received |
| In Progress | A support agent is actively working on it |
| Awaiting Reply | We've responded and are waiting for your reply |
| Resolved | The issue has been resolved |
| Closed | Ticket is closed (no recent activity) |

## Response Times

| Plan | Target Response Time |
|---|---|
| Free | 3–5 business days |
| Essential | 2–3 business days |
| Pro | 1 business day |
| Business+ | Same-day (priority support) |

<Tip>
  Include as much detail as possible: your plan, what you were doing when the issue occurred, any error messages, and screenshots if applicable. This helps us resolve tickets without back-and-forth.
</Tip>

## Other Ways to Reach Us

- **Email:** support@essaygen.ai

## Viewing Your Tickets

All submitted tickets are visible in the **Support** section of the app, organized by status. Click any ticket to view the conversation thread and reply.
